Before Shifts, there was Roster Genius - the universally praised tool that fixed rosters for Apple employees in over 350 Apple Retail stores around the world. Shifts for iPhone.

Shifts is the app for keeping your roster at your fingertips. Beautifully designed, it focuses on you and quickly helps you answer your number one question: when am I working next?

Share with your friends and family.

Shifts lets you share your roster with friends (or colleagues) to help you plan days off, organise the work carpool or notify family when your roster changes.

Easiest way to add shifts.

Adding shifts to Calendar apps is irritating and clunky. Shifts knows how rosters works and it learns to adopt your pattens to make adding a new roster or changing shifts super simple.
